Enhanced Carrom Board

HTML5 Canvas · Vanilla JavaScript · Local multiplayer · AI · Physics

Use mouse/touch: drag the striker back and release. P = pause, R = restart.

How to Play

  • Drag the striker to aim and release to shoot. Power shown by meter.
  • Pocket coins to score. Queen (red) gives +5 when pocketed.
  • Turns alternate when no coin is pocketed on the shot.
  • Single player uses a medium AI.
